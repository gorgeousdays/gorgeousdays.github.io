Wireshark常用过滤指令

过滤IP：ip.src eq x.x.x.x or ip.dst eq x.x.x.x

•过滤端口：tcp.port eq 80 or udp.port eq 80

•过滤协议：tcp/udp/http/tls/ssl/icmp/ftp/dns/ip

•http过滤模式：

​ •http.request.method == “GET”

​ •http.request.method == “POST”

​ •http.request.uri == “[路由地址]”

​ •[协议] contains “[关键字]”
